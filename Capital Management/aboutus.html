Mission & Vision

What drives us and where we're headed

Our Mission

To empower finance and operations teams with a reliable, intuitive platform that eliminates manual capital tracking, reduces errors, and gives decision-makers real-time visibility into fund allocation and asset utilization across every department.

Our Vision

To become the go-to enterprise solution for capital governance — where every rupee is accounted for, every asset is tracked, and every financial decision is backed by data. We envision a future where capital management is effortless and transparent.

Key Features

Everything you need to manage capital efficiently

Capital Master

Manage capital entries by type, financial year, and fund allocation. Track utilized vs. remaining funds with instant calculations.

Asset Master

Submit and manage asset requests with requester info, quantity, capital type, and approval routing — all in one place.

Unified Records List

View, search, filter, and export all capital and asset records in a single consolidated table with badge indicators.

Live Dashboard

At-a-glance stats, fund utilization bars, activity feed, and quick links — all auto-updated from your submitted records.

Microsoft SSO Auth

Secure login via Microsoft Azure Active Directory (MSAL). Role-based access ensures only authorized personnel can submit or view records.

CSV Export

Export any filtered view of records to CSV in one click for reporting, auditing, or sharing with stakeholders.
