Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Capital Management System?
The Capital Management System is a comprehensive solution for managing capital assets, tracking investments, and optimizing financial resources. It provides real-time dashboards, detailed reporting, and secure data management.
How do I add a new capital entry?
Navigate to the Capital Master module from the dashboard or sidebar, click on "Add New Capital Entry," fill in the required details, and submit the form. The entry will be immediately reflected in your capital allocations.
Can I export reports from the dashboard?
Yes, the dashboard provides comprehensive reporting features with options to view and download capital allocation reports, asset summaries, and financial statements in various formats.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade security with encrypted data storage, secure access controls, and regular security audits to ensure your capital management data is always protected.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team through the "Contact Support" option in the sidebar or by using the Contact Us button in the top navigation. Our team typically responds within 24 hours.
What is Asset Master used for?
Asset Master allows you to create, update, and monitor assets and equipment across your organization. You can track asset allocation, manage resource distribution, and maintain detailed asset records.
